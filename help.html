HELP & ADVICE

Find answers to common questions about our products, ordering process, and delivery.

How do I calculate how much concrete I need?

Use our Concrete Calculator tool to estimate the volume required for your project. Simply select your shape, enter the dimensions, and the tool will calculate the minimum volume in cubic metres (m³). We recommend adding 5–10% for wastage.

What concrete grades do you supply?

We supply a full range of ready-mix concrete grades from C20 to C70 MPa, including standard, sulphate-resistant, high-strength, and weather-adapted mixes engineered for Saudi Arabia's climate.

How do I book a concrete delivery?

Use the 'Book Concrete' form on our website, or call your nearest plant directly. Our team will confirm the order and schedule a delivery time that fits your project schedule.

What is the minimum order quantity?

The minimum order for ready-mix concrete is typically 1.5 m³. For smaller volumes, please contact your nearest plant to discuss available options.

How far in advance do I need to book?

We recommend booking at least 24–48 hours in advance for standard orders. For large volumes or specialised mixes, please allow 3–5 business days. Contact us to check availability.

Do you supply precast concrete and building blocks?

Yes. In addition to ready-mix, Jehan Holding Group manufactures precast concrete elements and certified building blocks. Contact us to discuss your requirements and get a quotation.
