ABOUT JEHAN HOLDING GROUP

Since 1996, Jehan Holding Group has been a trusted name in Saudi Arabia's construction materials industry. We manufacture and supply Ready-Mix Concrete, Precast Concrete, and Building Blocks — built to the standards that contractors, developers, and homebuilders across the Kingdom rely on.

What sets us apart isn't just what we produce, but how we do it. We are transparent about our materials, precise in our mixes, and honest with every client. Whether you're a homeowner laying a foundation or a contractor delivering a mega-infrastructure project, we take the time to understand your project before we recommend a solution.

Our product range covers everything from high-strength concrete (50–70 MPa) to sulphate-resistant and weather-adapted mixes — engineered for Saudi Arabia's demanding environment.

At Jehan, quality is not a promise. It's our track record.
